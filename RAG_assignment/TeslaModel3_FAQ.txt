Tesla Model 3 - Frequently Asked Questions
Q1: How do I order a Tesla vehicle?
A1: Visit Tesla's online configurator, customize your car, and submit an order.

Q2: Can I request a test drive?
A2: Yes, schedule a test drive through Tesla's website under the 'Test Drive' section.

Q3: What payment methods does Tesla accept?
A3: Tesla accepts credit/debit cards, ACH bank transfers, and financing options.

Q4: Where can I view my final invoice?
A4: Log in to your Tesla account and navigate to 'Orders & Payments'.

Q5: How do I submit my final payment?
A5: Use the payment portal in your Tesla account or follow instructions in your order confirmation.
