Dell XPS 13 - Frequently Asked Questions
Q1: How to run a diagnostic test?
A1: Use Dell SupportAssist or pre-boot diagnostics via F12 menu.

Q2: What are best practices for laptop maintenance?
A2: Keep vents clear, update drivers regularly, and use a surge protector.

Q3: How to reset BIOS settings?
A3: Restart, press F2 to enter BIOS, then choose 'Load Defaults'.

Q4: How to perform a hard reset?
A4: Power off, disconnect power, hold power button for 15 seconds.

Q5: How to update Dell drivers?
A5: Download from Dell's support site or use SupportAssist.
