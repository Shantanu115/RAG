Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra - Frequently Asked Questions
- How to transfer data from an iPhone?
  Use Samsung Smart Switch app via cable or wireless.

- How to use S Pen features?
  Air Actions can be accessed by holding the S Pen remote button.

- How to enable Expert RAW camera mode?
  Open Camera app, swipe to 'More', then select 'Expert RAW'.

- What battery optimization settings can I use?
  Go to Settings > Battery and device care > Battery > More battery settings.

- How to secure my device with Samsung Knox?
  Knox is enabled by default; configure under Settings > Biometrics and security.
