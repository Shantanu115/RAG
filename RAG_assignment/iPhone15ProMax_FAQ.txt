iPhone 15 Pro Max - Frequently Asked Questions
Q1: Where is the SIM tray located?
A1: On the left side, at the bottom below the volume buttons.

Q2: How do I use Dynamic Island?
A2: Dynamic Island appears at the top display cutout and adapts to notifications and Live Activities.

Q3: How do I enable Always-On display?
A3: Go to Settings > Display & Brightness and toggle on Always On display.

Q4: What is Action Button?
A4: The Action Button replaces the mute switch; you can customize it for various functions under Settings > Action Button.

Q5: How do I access the LiDAR scanner features?
A5: Apps like Measure and augmented reality experiences use the LiDAR scanner automatically when launched.
